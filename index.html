BootStrap Progress: 100%

Bootstrap uses reusable codes written in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It is a free and open-source front end web development framework to help you create responsive websites and web applications. Bootstrap is fast, easy to setup and no preprocessor needed. However, there are weaknesses to using Bootstrap. Bootstrap lacks unique visual style. Based on my observation, the style is not super appealing. Another weakness is the bloat of unused components. Overall, Bootstrap saves a lot of time whereas codes are already made. However, I noticed that using bootstrap required a lot of style overriding and overwriting files which can lead to a lot of time spent designing and coding a website. Compared to other Media Queries such as Flexbox and Skeleton system, Bootstrap is harder because you have to incorporate three codes (HTML, CSS * JavaScript) instead of just HTML and CSS.

ARTS AND CULTURE

Arts and culture are alive at Winters. Nearly every day brings theater and dance performances, concerts, art exhibits and visiting lecturers. And that's just on campus. Thanks to our location in Philadelphia, you also have easy access to the citys world-class museums, art galleries and performing arts venues.

CLUBS AND ORGANIZATIONS

Winters is home to hundreds of student-run clubs and organizations covering a wide variety of personal and professional interests. Each gives you the chance to become involved in campus life and connect with students who have similar interests. Learn more about Winters's clubs and organizations.

VARIETY

"Variety" is the best word to describe the housing and dining options available at Winters. We offer nine residence halls, various living arrangements and numerous dining options and meal plans from which to choose. Learn more about housing and dining.
